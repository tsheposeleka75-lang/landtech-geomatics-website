About Landtech Geomatics

Landtech Geomatics (Pty) Ltd is a South African sustainability, data intelligence, and programme coordination company. We design, train, and support scalable community investment models that enhance water security, energy resilience, recycling participation, and local entrepreneurship.

Holistic Approach

We operate at the intersection of renewable energy, water systems, circular economy solutions, GIS intelligence, and programme design.

Community Focused

Through our flagship LTG Green Cycle Programme, communities and entrepreneurs collectively invest in essential infrastructure.

Scalable Solutions

We create replicable, governable, and scalable systems supported by entrepreneurs, community structures, and impact-driven partnerships.

🎯 Our Vision

To build resilient, self-sustaining communities through data-driven infrastructure, green enterprise development, and inclusive investment models.

💡 Our Mission

To design and coordinate scalable community solutions that provide access to energy, water, skills, and economic opportunity while protecting the environment.
